Project Overview

This research project focuses on developing novel quantum algorithms for optimization problems in machine learning. Our team is investigating the potential of quantum computing to accelerate complex computational tasks and improve the efficiency of existing algorithms.

Key Objectives:

  • Develop quantum algorithms for machine learning optimization
  • Implement and test on quantum simulators
  • Analyze performance improvements over classical methods
  • Publish findings in top-tier journals
